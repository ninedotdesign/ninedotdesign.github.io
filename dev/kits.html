Xively Jumpstart Kit: ARM® mbed Edition

Xively and ARM have partnered to provide you the hardware, software and services you need to accelerate your ideas into connected products.  The ARM mbed starter kit is available today and has everything needed to prototype and connect to Xively Cloud Services™.

NXP LPC1768, ARM Cortex-M3

Specs: 96MHz, 32KB RAM, 512KB FLASH

I/O: Ethernet, USB Host/Device, 2xSPI, 2xI2C, 3xUART, CAN, 6xPWM, 6xADC, GPIO

Peripherals: see mbed Application Board

Power: 5V USB or 4.5-9V supply
