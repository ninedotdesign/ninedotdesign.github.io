  • Our Partner Programs

    Technology Partners

    Xively Technology Partner offerings seamlessly communicate with Xively Cloud Services and are used to create end-to-end IoT solutions. These include SaaS platforms, communication services provider (CSPs) services, analytics and tools software, system-on-chip solutions, wireless connectivity modules, and other components for complete IoT systems.

    Solution Providers

    Xively Solution Provider partners deliver the expertise and services necessary to design, architect, build, integrate, deploy and support connected products that leverage the Xively Cloud Services platform. These include systems integrators, software and hardware development houses, consultancies, and managed services companies.

    Industry Alliance Partners

    Xively Industry Alliance Partners are committed to furthering the commercial, technological, and societal benefits that are made possible by the Internet of Things. These include standards-organizations, educational institutions, government organizations and other for-profit and non-profit entities.
