Your connected business starts with Xively

The Internet of Things presents tremendous opportunities for businesses of all types and sizes, but knowing how to capitalize on them can be difficult. Whether you're creating a connected product or looking to optimize internal environments, Xively can help. We provide the IoT platform, applications, consulting and partners needed to empower 360-degree interactions designed to transform your business.

Focus on innovation instead of infrastructure

Our IoT cloud platform and open source libraries, out-of-the-box applications and consulting services simplify building your vision and shortcut your development time. Now, your business is free to focus on its core innovation.

Accelerate time-to-market

Xively's "Best-of-Breed" approach and robust partner ecosystem enable a wide range of options, speeding time-to-market while improving business outcomes. Through integration with vendors such as Salesforce.com, ARM and many more, Xively delivers a complete IoT-based solution, without reinventing the wheel.
