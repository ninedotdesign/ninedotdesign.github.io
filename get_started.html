Get Started with Xively

Xively provides the platform, tools, services and support needed to create and manage compelling connected products and services on the Internet of Things.

Business Services

Xively understands the business and technology of the Internet of Things and delivers the consulting services you need to create and deploy your vision for a connected product or solution. Together, we can help you drive new business quickly and at a lower total cost of ownership.

Partner Network

Whether you are interested in becoming a Technology Alliances partner that delivers the hardware or software building blocks or a Solution Provider that offers consulting, design and project management services for their customer, Xively has businesses that want what you provide.
